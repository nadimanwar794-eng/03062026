---
name: Study coin deferral
description: Study-earned coins are accumulated outside the user profile and paid together when Home opens.
---

Study-mode coin rewards must enter the shared pending study pool first; only the Home flow may consume that pool and update the spendable credit balance. Points/totalScore can still update during study.

**Why:** Immediate balance writes caused separate study modes to pay at different times and made combined session rewards prone to duplicate crediting.

**How to apply:** Keep non-study rewards (store, referral, redeem, gifts) immediate. For study rewards, defer coins by user ID and let the Home payout consume the pool exactly once.
